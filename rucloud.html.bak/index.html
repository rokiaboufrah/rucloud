The story behind ru.Cloud

"I didn't start a brand.
I found a way to survive."

ru.Cloud was born in 2023 — not from a business plan, but from pain, resilience, and an unshakeable belief that beauty can be built from the darkest places.

Every stitch carries a memory. Every silhouette, a chapter of a story that refused to end quietly. This brand is my voice — and now, it can be yours too.
